Guillermo del Toro

Born October 9, 1964, Guillermo del Toro is a Mexican film director, writer, producer and overall a gift to cinema and the world over. He is the director of numerous critically-acclaimed films such as Cronos, The Devil's Backbone, Pan's Labyrinth, and The shape of Water.
